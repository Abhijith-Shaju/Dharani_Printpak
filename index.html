/ Systems Catalog

Structural Lineup

Rigid
Boxes

Electronics, Luxury Gifts
Corporate Gift & Premium Retail

// CUSTOM_SPEC

Rigid boxes are designed for products that require strong protection and refined presentation. Made from high-density board and finished with premium papers, they offer durability along with an elegant look and feel.

We manufacture both classic rigid boxes and collapsible rigid boxes, delivering luxury appeal with flexible construction options — including space-saving flat-pack designs when required.

  • Magnetic Closures
  • Telescopic & Lift-Off Lids
  • Foil Stamping & Embossing
  • Custom Sizes & Finishes
  • Collapsible Construction

Mono Cartons

FMCG & Retail, Beauty & Pharma
Food, Beverage & E-commerce Goods

// CUSTOM_SPEC

Monocartons are designed for retail products that require clear branding and structural accuracy. Crafted from high-quality paperboard, they ensure sharp printing, consistent quality, and dependable protection.

We manufacture custom monocartons engineered for scalable production while maintaining premium visual impact across every unit.

  • UV Coating
  • Lamination (Matte & Gloss)
  • Metallic Effects
  • Custom Die-Cut Structures
  • High-Precision Printing

Paperboard
Bags

FMCG & Retail, Beauty & Pharma
Food, Beverage & E-commerce Goods

// CUSTOM_SPEC

Paperboard bags are designed for brands that require durability, reusability, and refined retail presentation. Manufactured from strong paperboards and finished with premium printing and surface options.

We produce custom paperboard bags in a variety of structural formats, including reinforced bases and handle options — combining everyday functionality with elevated visual appeal.

  • Rope or Ribbon Handles
  • Textured & Specialty Papers
  • Multiple Structural Formats
  • Reinforced Bases
  • Custom Sizes & Printing Finishes
